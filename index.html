Research Highlights

My research has focused on studies of solid-state materials using computational methods.

During my PhD program, I studied the so-called high entropy alloys. These alloys are mostly chemically disordered on a simple crystal lattice (body-centered cubic, face-centered cubic, etc.), which makes it particularly difficult to simulate using first principles methods.
